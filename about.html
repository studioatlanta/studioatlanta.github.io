About US

Daniel Martin

Daniel Martin, a seasoned photographer with over 40 years of experience, has earned over 60 awards, including recognition in magazines like Maxim, Hooters, and Teen. He photographed Vice-President George H. Bush and has excelled in customer service. A member of the Professional Photographers of America, he also teaches photography classes.
In 1992, Daniel founded Hollywood Creations Photography, the first at-home glamour photography company in the U.S., running two successful locations in Atlanta and Charlotte. He's captured over 1000 weddings, models, families, and more. Daniel's expertise spans food, corporate events, album covers, and charity work. Today, as the owner of Studio Atlanta Photography, he remains a top-rated Atlanta photographer known for professionalism and award-winning images.

Denise Martin
